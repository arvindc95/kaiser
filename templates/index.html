Kaiser is a trick-taking game played with two teams of two players. The game is played with 32 cards - 7 to Ace in every suit except hearts and spades, where the 7's are replaced with the 5 of Hearts and the 3 of Spades, respectively.

Bidding starts clockwise from the dealer. Players bid based on how many points they can make, knowing that the 5 is worth 5 points, the 3 is worth -3 points, and that every trick is worth 1 point. The minimum bid is 7. Bidding no trump can get you double points - or lose them.
